#teamqueercode

-------

We are a community of LGBTQ (web, app, software, etc) developers looking to:

  • Build a strong, inclusive, non-judgemental network and space where all voices are honored and heard.
  • Share thoughts, ideas, inspiration, work we're currently doing, etc - the sky's the limit!
  • Get help with any development issues we're having.
  • Mentor and learn from each other.
  • Have some FUN!

#teamqueercode is all about creating community and ensuring that the community that we develop is ever evolving to include all voices in the LGBTQ community. We're really looking to build this initiative from the ground up, with each member fully participating in the direction and construciton of the group.

Thus, we're starting small with Slack. We feel like this is the best way for people to begin getting together and iterating over what #teamqueercode could ultimately become. Let's focus on building the community first, and then we can figure out what kind of digital / physical footprint we want to have in the world. If this jives with you, please click the button, submit your email, and join the fun!
